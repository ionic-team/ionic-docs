Supports:

 - **iOS** version 7.0 or higher.
 - **Android** version 2.2 (API level 8) or higher
   - with Google Play client version 3.9.16 or higher
 - **Windows** Store/Phone 8.1 or higher
 - **Windows 10 Mobile**
 - **macOS** version 10
 - **Xbox One**
   - (and any platform supporting Microsoft's UWP)
